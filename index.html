Plano Senior HS Introduces

2016 Summer Wildcat Speech & Debate Camp - Available to Middle and High School Students


A Winning Tradition

At Plano Senior, our school consistently produces results. Within the last 20 years, Plano Senior has won the TFA state 15 times. We have produced a plethora state-winners in a vast variety of events, including in Public Forum Debate, Congressional Debate, Extemporaneous Speaking, Dramatic Interp, Original Oratory, Humerous Interp, and much more.

By utilizing the same techniques and strategies we implement within our classroom setting at the Wildcat Speech & Debate Camp, we will assure improvements in the respective abilities of attendees. Our proven staff has substantial expertise in aiding our attendees and assuring we put our students down the correct path.

Curriculum & Lessons

Debate - Argumentation

Learn how to formulate and utilize proper argumentation skills through several drills, teachings, and techniques.

Debate - Speaking

Train your communications skills to master the art of speaking by decreasing fluency breaks while maximizing time allocation during speeches.

Debate - Case Writing

Gain crucial skills to construct efficient and intellectual cases that will bolster your ability to win your rounds.

Speech - Writing

Improve your writing skills by learning the fundemental elements of a paper, play, or speech, within your respective event.

Speech - Performance

Gain several hours of practicing and performing your speech/play in front of highly-qualified mentors and leaders who will provide critiquing and constructive-criticism.

Speech - Ideas

Learn to forumlate ande execute your ideas for speeches/plays that will aid you in constructing the perfect performance to win tournaments.

The Camp Experience


Instructors

The Wildcat Speech & Debate Camp provides the opportunity to have a learning environment with highly-qualified instructors with several opporuntity for one on one practice. Instructors are trained in their respective events and are determined to aid students to succeed and aquire proper communication skills that will aid students in their careers within Speech & Debate

Hours of Practice

The Wildcat Speech & Debate camp provides hours of practice and hands-on training that will allow students to perform and work in front of their peers and instructors to garner the experience of competition and tournaments.
